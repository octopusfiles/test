Sanjeev Jain and Alok Sarin (Eds.)
Sage Publications, 2018.

Read an excerpt here.

The absence of psychological narratives of the Partition is a deafening silence. Guilt, denial, suppression, acting out? Writers, literary theorists, feminists, political scientists, and psychiatrists discuss the possible reasons for the silence.
